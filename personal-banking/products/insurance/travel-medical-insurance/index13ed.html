Ideal for occasional travellers

Are you going on a trip? See if the Per Trip Plan1 is the best fit for you.

  • You won’t have to deal with out of pocket costs ‑ we’ll take care of upfront payments of eligible medical emergency expenses2
  • Comprehensive medical emergency coverage up to $5 million with no deductible required
  • 24/7 Emergency Assistance world wide
  • Couple and family coverage available
  • For trips 212 days or less3

Ideal for frequent travellers

Go away often? An Annual Plan1 is our most convenient protection. See if it’s right for you.

  • Allows unlimited trips of 9,17,30 or 60 days during the year
  • Comprehensive medical emergency coverage up to $5 million with no deductible required
  • 24/7 Emergency Assistance world wide
  • Couple and family coverage available

Exclusive for Snowbirds 55 years and older

Travelling for 30 days or more? Consider the 55+ Extended Stay Plan1.

  • Comprehensive medical emergency coverage up to $5 million even with some pre-existing conditions4
  • No out of pocket costs for eligible medical emergency expenses2
  • 24/7 Emergency Assistance world wide
  • Exclusive benefits included, just for Snowbirds

View Certificate of Insurance 

View Distribution Guide (for residents of Quebec only) 

Protect your trip investment1.

 

  • Before you go ‑ We’ll cover eligible non-refundable travel expenses, like trip deposits and flight change fees, if your trip is cancelled because of a covered cause
  • While on your trip ‑ We’ll cover your trip home or to your next destination if your plans change due to an unexpected covered cause
  • Includes reimbursement for meals, accommodation, taxi fare, and other expenses if your trip is delayed or interrupted due to a covered cause
  • Provides coverage for medical and non-medical causes

TD Travel Medical Insurance (Per Trip Plan, Annual Plan and TD 55+ Extended Stay Travel Medical Insurance) is underwritten by TD Life Insurance Company. TD Trip Cancellation and Trip Interruption Insurance is underwritten by TD Life Insurance Company (medical covered causes) and TD Home and Auto Insurance Company (non-medical covered causes). Medical and claim assistance, claim payment and administration services under the Policy are provided by our Administrator.

1 Coverage may be subject to exclusions and limitations. Refer to your Certificate of Insurance for full details of coverage.

2 Eligible medical emergency expenses are defined in your Certificate of Insurance. Payment of eligible medical emergency expenses is subject to our Administrator’s approval.

3 Up to 212 days but not longer than the maximum number of days allowed under your Government Health Insurance Plan (GHIP) for travel outside of Canada.

4 Answering a health-related questionnaire is required to determine eligibility for this product. Depending on your eligibility, all pre-existing conditions will be covered.
