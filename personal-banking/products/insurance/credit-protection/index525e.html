Mortgage Credit Protection

If you die, get a terminal illness or suffer a covered accidental dismemberment1, TD Mortgage Life Insurance can pay up to $1,000,0002 towards your:

  • Outstanding TD mortgage balance, less any arrears
  • Discharge/penalty fees
  • Overdrawn balance on your property tax account
  • Interest owing

As an enhancement, you can add optional TD Mortgage Critical Illness Insurance which can pay up to $1,000,0003 if you are diagnosed with Cancer (life threatening), Acute Heart Attack, or Stroke1.

  • We offer competitive group rates, and discounts may apply4.

TD Line of Credit Insurance

If you die or suffer a covered accidental dismemberment1, TD Line of Credit Life Insurance can pay up to $300,000 towards:

  • Your unpaid line of credit balance less any arrears
  • Interest owing
  • Discharge or penalty fees

As an enhancement, you can add optional TD Line of Credit Critical Illness Insurance2 which can pay up to $300,000 if you are diagnosed with Cancer (life threatening), Acute Heart Attack, or Stroke1.

  • We offer competitive group rates, and discounts may apply3.

TD Loan Credit Protection

TD Balance Protection Plus insurance

TD Balance Protection Plus is an optional product designed to help you deal with your TD Credit Card(s) payment obligations in the event of a covered Involuntary Unemployment, Loss of Self-Employment Income, Total Disability, Disability requiring Hospitalization, Loss of Life, Dismemberment or Critical Illness.

TD Balance Protection Plus

  • Maximum amount of insurance: $25,000
  • Monthly Premium Rate per $100 of average daily balance: $1.20, plus applicable taxes. Premium is only charged on the first $10,000 of average daily balance.
  • Monthly Premium rate is reduced on statement date after you turn 66 to $0.59 per $100 of average daily balance.
  • One-time benefit payments to your credit card should you die, suffer a Dismemberment, Critical Illness (Acute Heart Attack, Cancer Life threatening or Stroke).
  • Monthly benefit of 10% of your eligible outstanding balance for the following coverages: Involuntary Unemployment, Loss of Self-Employment Income and Total Disability
  • Disability requiring Hospitalization benefit of 10% of eligible outstanding balance for hospitalization of at least 24 hours and of the remaining eligible outstanding balance if the hospitalization continues for more than 30 days.
  • Includes spousal coverage for all coverages above, including Disability Requiring Hospitalization. Involuntary Unemployment and Loss of Self-Employment Income are excluded for spousal coverage.

All insurance coverage will terminate for you and, if applicable, your spouse on the next Account Statement Date after you turn 66. The Life coverage for you and your spouse will be replaced with Accidental Death At 66 coverage, regardless of your spouse’s age.

For more information or to enroll today, call 1-866-315-90691-866-315-9069 and make our optional credit card balance insurance products work for you!

TD Business Credit Life Insurance

TD Business Credit Life Insurance1 provides life and accidental dismemberment protection, and can pay up to $1 million per Insured Person2 towards:

  • Your outstanding balance of the insured authorized business credit products
  • Interest owing
  • Any surplus life benefit will be paid to your business

We offer competitive group rates, and lower rates are available for non-smokers3.
