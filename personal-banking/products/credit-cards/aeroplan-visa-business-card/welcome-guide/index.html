Welcome to your new TD® Aeroplan® Visa* Business Card

Quick steps to getting started and making the most of your card

  • Learn more about the features and benefits of your TD Aeroplan Visa Business Card by calling 1-800-983-8472 to speak with a Business Credit Card specialist. Don't forget to sign your Card!
  • Complete your profile on aeroplan.com and access relevant offers, check your miles balance, review transactions, and manage your rewards.
  • Update your Card as the Primary Card on your favorite mobile apps for auto-reloads, food delivery and transportation to enjoy convenience for your everyday purchases.
  • Learn how to add your Card to Apple Pay if you have an iPhone, to make convenient, everyday business payments with just a tap of your phone.
  • Set up regularly recurring bill payments on your Account with participating merchants to save time and avoid late fees with bills paid automatically on their due dates.
  • Register for online statements on EasyWeb to save time and stay organized.

Help and assistance

  • If your TD Credit Card is damaged or not working, you can request a replacement card online through EasyWeb, through the TD app using your mobile device, or by phone at 1-800-983-8472.
  • If your TD Credit Card has been lost or stolen, you must immediately report this to us by phone at: 1-800-983-8472.
  • Fast, reliable access to cash. You can ask for an emergency Cash Advance of up to $500 in emergency funds (subject to your available credit limit), at over one million ATMs worldwide, at all TD Canada Trust branches and at select financial institutions around the world.
  • Getting information when you need it. The TD Credit Card Contact Centre can help you with important information, such as the location of the nearest Visa member office for Cash Advances or contact numbers for embassies and consulates.

Ways to Pay

Cover business expenses with TD Visa Cheques

If you need to pay for a business expense that can’t be charged to a credit card, use TD Visa Cheques1 anywhere cheques are accepted and the amount of your business expense will be processed directly to your TD Aeroplan Visa Business Card Account as a Cash Advance.
Call to request yours: 1-800-983-8472

Pay faster with Visa payWave*

Visa payWave technology is a secure, convenient, contactless way to pay for small-dollar purchases at participating retailers. It’s faster and more convenient than cash - and your Card never leaves your hand.

Save time with Apple Pay

Apple Pay is accepted in a growing number of shops, restaurants, and other retailers. Many apps have also added Apple Pay to their accepted methods of payment. Just look for the Apple Pay or contactless payment symbols at the check-out.

Shop online simply with Visa Checkout

Visa Checkout is an easier way to pay online. Sign up for Visa Checkout, a service available from Visa that simplifies the online checkout process. It's available anywhere you see the Visa Checkout logo.

Earn Aeroplan Miles

With your TD® Aeroplan®Visa* Business Card, you earn Aeroplan Miles on every business Purchase you make on your Card. Earn Aeroplan Miles faster 4 ways:

  • Earn 1.5 miles2 for every $1 you spend on business travel and gas Purchases, using your Card.
  • Earn 1 mile3 for every $1 you spend on all other business Purchases, using your Card.
  • Earn miles twice4 when you pay with your Card and present your Aeroplan membership card at over 150 Aeroplan partner brands and at 120+ online retailers via the Aeroplan eStore. Learn where you can earn miles twice at aeroplan.com.
  • Your Aeroplan Miles will not expire as long as you are a TD Aeroplan Visa Business Cardholder.
  • TIP: Check for Aeroplan partner offers regularly at aeroplan.com/promotions so you can take advantage of bonus Aeroplan Miles.

Earn miles faster and get to your travel plans sooner!

Add Additional Cards

Need more cards for your business? Add additional Cardholders6 and watch your miles balance rise! Earn miles every time they make business Purchases on their Cards.

Earn more Aeroplan Miles with regularly recurring bill payments

Set up Pre-Authorized Payments on your account for business expenses like utilities, or phone bills. You'll earn Aeroplan Miles and your bills are paid automatically on their due dates.

Achieve Aeroplan status

Aeroplan status benefits and privileges.

Top-earning Aeroplan members can achieve status in the Aeroplan program and take advantage of special recognition in the form of exclusive flight reward benefits, bonus miles and unique privileges.
Three levels of qualification:

  • Aeroplan silver: when you earn 25,000 eligible miles per calendar year
  • Aeroplan black: when you earn 50,000 eligible miles per calendar year
  • Aeroplan diamond: when you earn 100,000 eligible miles per calendar year

IMPORTANT: Your last cycle date in December is the cut-off date to use your TD Aeroplan Credit Card to earn miles towards Aeroplan status in the current year. Anything earned later will count towards the following year’s Aeroplan status.

Want to know more? Visit aeroplan.com/status

Get any seat

With a choice of two flight reward options, Aeroplan members get access to any seat, at any time, on all Air Canada® flights.

  • Fixed Mileage Flight Rewards® are seats on Air Canada and over 25 partner airlines, offered exclusively7 to Aeroplan members at fixed mileage levels.
  • Market Fare Flight Rewards® offer access to any seat, at any time, on all Air Canada flights, at mileage levels that vary based on market fares at time of booking.
  • Bottom line, on average Aeroplan members flew for less than they would have with leading financial institutions’ travel rewards programs in Canada on the 1.9 million flights redeemed last year8.

Market Fare Flight Rewards

TIP: When you can’t find the availability you are looking for with Fixed Mileage Flight Rewards, check Market Fare Flight Rewards, which give you access to all available seats on all Air Canada operated flights9!

Pay for taxes with miles on your Credit Card

When you redeem Aeroplan Miles for an Aeroplan flight reward, you have the flexibility to use your miles to cover the taxes, fees and surcharges associated with your flight reward booking10. It’s easy: book your flight and you’ll be given the option to cover the entire amount using either your miles or a charge to your TD Aeroplan Visa Business Card. The choice is yours!

Your TD® Aeroplan®Visa* Business Card comes with travel perks

Air Canada Benefits for the Primary Business Cardholder11

When you travel using an Aeroplan flight reward, redeemed using miles from your Aeroplan account on flights operated by Air Canada®, Air Canada Rouge® or under the Air Canada Express® banner, you’ll receive:

  • One complimentary first checked bag11. As the Primary Business Cardholder, you’ll receive a complimentary first checked bag, up to 23kg (50lb).
  • Priority check-in and priority boarding11. You and your companions travelling on the same reservation and checking-in at the same time can enjoy the convenience of priority check-in and priority boarding.
  • Air Canada passengers will now be boarding according to zones clearly indicated on the Air Canada issued boarding pass. Your priority boarding benefit makes you eligible for Zone 2 priority boarding.
  • One annual One-Time Guest Access Benefit11 to an Air Canada Maple Leaf LoungeTM. Each year, you automatically receive one (1) annual One-Time Guest Access Benefit to an Air Canada Maple Leaf Lounge.

Simply present yourself at the lounge with your valid, same-day Aeroplan flight reward boarding pass and your TD Aeroplan Visa Business Card.

Be sure to travel with your TD Aeroplan Visa Business Card

You may be required to show your Card to an Air Canada agent to receive Air Canada benefits.

Eligibility information

In order to be eligible to use Air Canada benefits, be sure to travel using an Aeroplan flight reward redeemed with miles from your own Aeroplan account. Please allow two weeks following activation of your Card for these Air Canada benefits to be in effect.

Travelling with more guests?

For a nominal fee, the Primary Business Cardholder who is using a Maple Leaf Lounge One-Time Guest Access Benefit is permitted up to a maximum of six paid guests in the lounge per visit, pending space availability. Access is subject to Air Canada Maple Leaf Lounge terms and conditions and may not be available due to unforeseen circumstances. For clarity, this paid guest product is not part of the suite of Air Canada benefits offered along with a TD Aeroplan Visa Business Card. Enjoy a suite of insurance benefits with your Card

  • Flight/Trip Delay Insurance12
    Take the stress out of travel delays. If your trip is delayed more than four hours, you are covered for up to $500 for reasonable and eligible hotel, motel or restaurant expenses whenever you use your Card and/or Aeroplan Miles to cover the full cost of your transportation.
  • Common Carrier Travel Accident Insurance13
    Travel protected in planes, trains, and more. You, your spouse and dependent children are insured for up to $500,000 while travelling on a common carrier (for example, a bus, ferry, plane, or train) whenever you use your Card and/or Aeroplan Miles to cover the full cost of your transportation.
  • Delayed and Lost Baggage Insurance14
    Shop for delayed items. If your baggage is delayed more than six hours, each insured person is covered for emergency purchases of essentials such as clothing and toiletries. Coverage is also provided if your baggage is lost by a Common Carrier. Use your business Card and/or Aeroplan Miles to cover the full cost of the tickets. Each insured person is covered up to an overall maximum of $1,000 per trip.
  • Auto Rental Collision/Loss Damage Insurance15
    Simply use your business Card and/or Aeroplan Miles to pay for the full cost of an eligible car rental, and get the Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) coverage at no additional cost for up to 48 consecutive days. Please note, some rental agencies may require you to buy the CDW coverage from them. It is important to contact the rental agency before reserving a car to verify their policies.

Explore your TD® Aeroplan®Visa* Business Card fraud security features

Protecting the security of your Card should always be a priority. That’s why your TD Aeroplan Visa Business
Card comes with features that help protect you when you shop online and in-person.

Security Features

  • Shop online and in person securely with Visa Zero Liability – included free with your Card. Be protected in the event unauthorized transactions are made on your Account when you've protected your PIN.
  • Shop with confidence with chip & PIN technology. Chip & PIN technology provides an added level of security through the use of a Personal Identification Number (PIN).
  • Verified by Visa* provides you with increased security and convenience when you shop online.

Shop with confidence

You automatically have access to Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection22, which includes coverage for most eligible new items you’ve purchased with your Card:

  • Repair, replacement, or reimbursement should eligible items be stolen or damaged within 90 days of purchase
  • Double your warranty, up to 12 additional months if the eligible item came with a manufacturer's warranty valid in Canada

Personal Identification Number (PIN) Security Tips

  • Choose a PIN you can remember that's difficult to guess. Don't use a phone number or birthday
  • Never write down your PIN.
  • Protect your PIN and use your hand or body to shield the keypad when you enter your PIN.
  • Never let anyone else know or use your PIN or use your Card.
  • Make transactions only when and where you feel secure. If in doubt, don’t use your Card.

Reset your PIN easily and conveniently at a TD Canada Trust Branch or 24/7 at any TD Canada Trust Green Machine (ATM).

Fraud Protection (24-hour)

Our protection measures help protect you from credit card fraud. In the event that we detect unusual activity relating to your Account:

  • You may receive a phone call from a TD Credit Card Customer Service Representative asking you to confirm that you performed a transaction on your TD Aeroplan Visa Business Card or Account.
  • During a transaction, a merchant may receive an electronic message requesting that they contact the TD Credit Card Contact Centre and allow you to speak with one of our Customer Service Representatives.
  • Your Card may be temporarily blocked until we can contact you for verification.
  • You can now automatically receive TD Fraud Alerts to your mobile phone any time we suspect suspicious activity on your TD Aeroplan Visa* Business Card. Learn more.
