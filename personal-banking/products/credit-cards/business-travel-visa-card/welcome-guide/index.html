Your new TD Business Travel Visa* Card

Quick steps to getting started and making the most of your business card

  • Learn more about the features and benefits of your TD Business Travel Visa* Card by calling 1-800-983-8472 to speak with a Business Credit Card specialist. Don't forget to sign your business Card!
  • Register at TDRewards.com to track how many TD Rewards Points you've earned and discover the redemption options available to you.
  • Update your business Card as the Primary Card on your favorite mobile apps like auto-reloads and transportation to enjoy convenience for your everyday business purchases1
  • Learn how to add your business Card to Apple Pay if you have an iPhone, to make convenient, everyday business payments with just a tap of your phone.
  • Set up regularly recurring bill payments on your TD Business Travel Visa Account with participating merchants to save time and avoid late fees with bills paid automatically on their due dates.
  • Register for online statements on EasyWeb to save time and stay organized.
  • Download the TD app to view your business Credit Card Account almost anywhere. Plus, you can bank at home, or on the go.

Ways to Pay

Cover business expenses with TD Visa Cheques

If you need to pay for a business expense that can’t be charged to a credit card, use TD Visa Cheques2 anywhere cheques are accepted and the amount of your business expense will be processed directly to your TD Business Travel Visa Card Account as a Cash Advance.

Call to request yours: 1-800-983-8472

Pay with Visa payWave*

Visa payWave technology is a secure, convenient, contactless way to pay for small-dollar purchases at participating retailers. It’s faster and more convenient than cash - and your business Card never leaves your hand.

Save time with Apple Pay

Apple Pay is accepted in a growing number of shops, restaurants, and other retailers. Many apps have also added Apple Pay to their accepted methods of payment. Just look for the Apple Pay or contactless payment symbols at the check-out.

Shop online simply with Visa Checkout

Visa Checkout3 is an easier way to pay online. Sign up for Visa Checkout, a service available from Visa that simplifies the online checkout process. It's available anywhere you see the Visa Checkout logo.

Your TD Business Travel Visa* Card comes with travel perks

Travel Medical Insurance11

You have coverage without even having to charge the trip to your business Card! Leaving home for a day, a week or an extended vacation is a great way to leave your worries behind. To help you with the consequences of unforeseen events, your TD Business Travel Visa Card features Travel Medical Insurance, which includes:

  • Up to $2 Million comprehensive emergency medical coverage for when you are travelling outside of your province or territory, whether internationally or within Canada
  • No out-of-pocket costs for eligible emergency medical expenses
  • 24/7 emergency medical assistance
  • Coverage for the first 15 days of your trip if you are under 65 years of age. If you or your spouse is aged 65 or older, you are covered for the first 4 days of your trip. For longer trips, top-up coverage is available for purchase by calling 1-800-293-4941

Enjoy a suite of insurance benefits with your Card

  • Flight/Trip Delay Insurance12
    Take the stress out of travel delays. If your trip is delayed more than four hours, each insured person is covered for up to $500 for reasonable and eligible hotel, motel or restaurant expenses whenever you charge the full cost of your transportation to your business Card and/or TD Rewards Points.
  • Common Carrier Travel Accident Insurance12
    Purchase your full fare with your business Card and/or TD Rewards Points and you, your spouse and dependent children will be insured for up to $500,000 in case of an injury due to an accident while travelling on a common carrier (for example, a bus, ferry, plane, or train).
  • Delayed and Lost Baggage Insurance13
    Shop for delayed items. If your baggage is delayed more than six hours, each insured person is covered for emergency purchases of essentials such as clothing and toiletries. Coverage is also provided if your baggage is lost by a Common Carrier. Use your business Card and/or TD Rewards Points to cover the full cost of the tickets. Each insured person is covered up to an overall maximum of $1,000 per trip.
  • Auto Rental Collision/Loss Damage Insurance14
    Simply use your business Card and/or TD Rewards Points to pay for the full cost of an eligible car rental, and get the Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) coverage at no additional cost for up to 48 consecutive days. Please note, some rental agencies may require you to buy the CDW coverage from them. It is important to contact the rental agency before reserving a car to verify their policies.

Explore your TD Business Travel Visa* Card security and protection

Protecting the security of your business Card should always be a priority. That’s why your TD Business Travel Visa* Card comes with features that help protect you when you shop online and in-person.

Security Features

  • Shop online and in person securely with Visa Zero Liability and be protected in the event that unauthorized transactions are made on your Card. See your Cardholder Agreement for complete information on unauthorized transactions and your responsibilities including your responsibility to protect your PIN.
  • Shop with confidence with chip & PIN technology. Chip and PIN technology provides an added level of security through the use of a Personal Identification Number (PIN).
  • Verified by Visa* provides you with increased security and convenience when you shop online.

Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection23

You automatically have access to Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection, which includes coverage for most eligible new items you’ve purchased with your business Card:

  • Repair, replacement, or reimbursement should eligible items be stolen or damaged within 90 days of purchase
  • Double your warranty, up to 12 additional months if the eligible item came with a manufacturer's warranty valid in Canada

Personal Identification Number (PIN) Security Tips

  • Choose a PIN you can remember that's difficult to guess. Don't use a phone number or birthday.
  • Never write down your PIN.
  • Protect your PIN and use your hand or body to shield the keypad when you enter your PIN.
  • Never let anyone else know or use your PIN or use your business Card.
  • Make transactions only when and where you feel secure. If in doubt, don’t use your business Card.

Reset your PIN easily and conveniently at a TD Canada Trust Branch or 24/7 at any TD Canada Trust Green Machine (ATM).

Fraud Protection (24-hour)

Our protection measures help protect you from credit card fraud. In the event that we detect unusual activity relating to your Account:

  • You may receive a phone call from a TD Credit Card Customer Service Representative asking you to confirm that you, or an Additional User, performed a transaction on your TD Business Travel Visa* Card or Account.
  • During a transaction, a merchant may receive an electronic message requesting that they contact the TD Credit Card Contact Centre and allow you to speak with one of our Customer Service Representatives.
  • Your business Card may be temporarily blocked until we can contact you for verification.
