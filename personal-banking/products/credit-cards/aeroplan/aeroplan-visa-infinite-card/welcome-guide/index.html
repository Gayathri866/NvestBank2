Welcome to your new TD® Aeroplan® Visa Infinite* Card

Quick steps to getting started and making the most of your Card:

  • Activate your TD® Aeroplan®Visa Infinite* Card simply by calling the number on the sticker found on your new Card, then follow the prompts. Don't forget to sign your Card!
  • Register for EasyWeb Online banking to access and manage your Account online and sign up for online statements or, if you prefer to bank by phone, call EasyLine telephone banking at 1-866-222-3456.
  • Complete your online profile at aeroplan.com to access relevant offers, check your miles balance, review transactions, plus manage your rewards… all in one convenient place!
  • Set up regularly recurring bill payments on your Account through participating merchants to save time and avoid late fees, with bills paid automatically on their due dates. Plus, earn 1 Aeroplan mile for every $1 on regularly recurring bill payments set up on your Account.
  • You can add your Card as the Primary Card on your favourite retail apps, like food delivery and transportation apps. Earn Aeroplan miles on purchases made through those apps on your phone1.
  • Learn how to add your Card to Apple Pay if you have an iPhone, to make convenient, everyday payments with just a tap of your phone.
  • Learn how to add your Card to TD Mobile Payment through the TD app if you use an AndroidTM smartphone2, and you can pay safely and securely.
  • Download the TD app to view your Credit Card Account almost anywhere. Plus, you can bank at home, or on the go.

Earn Aeroplan miles on every Purchase

Earn Aeroplan miles faster with your TD® Aeroplan®Visa Infinite* Card:

  • Earn 1.5 miles3 for every $1 you charge to your Card on eligible gas, grocery and drugstore Purchases, and on eligible aircanada.com Purchases.
  • Earn 1 mile4 for every $1 you charge to your Card on all other Purchases.
  • Earn miles twice5 when you pay with your Card and provide your Aeroplan membership number at over 150 Aeroplan partner brands and at 150 online retailers via the Aeroplan eStore.

Check for Aeroplan partner offers regularly so you can take advantage of bonus Aeroplan miles.

Remember, your Aeroplan miles will not expire as long as you are a TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Cardholder.

Add Authorized User Cards

Earn miles faster and get to your travel plans sooner. Add up to three Authorized User Cards6 (additional fee(s) apply) and earn miles every time they use their Cards to make Purchases.

Earn Aeroplan miles with regularly recurring bill payments

Set up regularly recurring bill payments on your Account through participating merchants for things like TV or phone bills. Save time and avoid late fees when your bills are paid automatically on their due dates, and earn 1 Aeroplan mile for every $1 set up on your Account.

Achieve Aeroplan status

Receive special benefits and privileges as a member with Aeroplan status.
Top-earning Aeroplan members can achieve status in the Aeroplan program and take advantage of special recognition in the form of exclusive flight reward benefits, bonus miles and unique privileges.

Three levels of qualification:
Aeroplan silver: when you earn 25,000 eligible miles per calendar year
Aeroplan black: when you earn 50,000 eligible miles per calendar year
Aeroplan diamond: when you earn 100,000 eligible miles per calendar year

IMPORTANT: Your last cycle date in December is the cut-off date to use your TD Aeroplan Credit Card to earn miles towards Aeroplan status. Anything earned later will count towards the following year’s Aeroplan status.

Want to know more? Visit aeroplan.com/status

Get any seat

Aeroplan’s two flight reward options, Fixed Mileage Flight Rewards® and Market Fare Flight Rewards®, work together so that you have access to any seat, on all flights operated by Air Canada.

  • Fixed Mileage Flight Rewards: Flights operated by Air Canada and over 25 airline partners, exclusively offered to Aeroplan Members at fixed mileage levels.
  • Market Fare Flight Rewards: Access to all available seats on all Air Canada operated flights7. Mileage levels vary based on market fares at time of booking.

Market Fare Flight Rewards

When you can’t find the availability you are looking for with Fixed Mileage Flight Rewards, check Market Fare Flight Rewards, which give you access to all available seats on all Air Canada operated flights7!

You can also redeem for a one-way Fixed Mileage Flight Reward for 50% of the miles required for a round trip to all routes including international destinations.

Cover Taxes, Fees and Surcharges with miles or your Credit Card

You have the flexibility to use Aeroplan miles to cover the Taxes, Fees and Surcharges associated with your flight reward bookings8. Upon selection of your itinerary, Aeroplan will offer the flexibility to cover the entire amount using your miles or your credit card. The choice is yours! The amount of miles required is variable and may depend on the actual amount of Taxes, Fees and Surcharges specific to your chosen travel itinerary as well as other criteria.

Explore the insurance coverages included with your TD® Aeroplan®Visa Infinite* Card

Travel Medical Insurance9

You have coverage without even having to charge the trip to your Card! Leaving home for a day, a week or an extended vacation is a great way to leave your worries behind. To help you with the consequences of unforeseen events, your TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Card features Travel Medical Insurance, which includes:

  • Up to $1 Million comprehensive emergency medical coverage for when you are travelling outside of your province or territory, whether internationally or within Canada
  • No out-of-pocket costs for eligible emergency medical expenses
  • 24/7 emergency medical assistance
  • Coverage for the first 21 days of your trip if you are under 65 years of age. If you or your spouse is aged 65 or older, you are covered for the first 4 days of your trip. For longer trips, top-up coverage is available for purchase by calling 1-800-293-4941
  • Trip Cancellation/Trip Interruption Insurance10
    Breathe easy, before and during your trip. Use your Card and/or Aeroplan miles to cover the full cost of your trip, and you’ll have coverage for up to $1,500 per insured person to a maximum of $5,000 for all insured persons if you need to cancel your travel plans before you leave because of a covered cause; and up to $5,000 per insured person to a maximum of $25,000 for all insured per covered trip if you need to come home early due to a covered cause. If you require additional coverage, you may be eligible to increase your coverage for an additional premium by calling 1-800-293-4941.
  • Flight/Trip Delay Insurance11
    Take the stress out of travel delays. If your trip is delayed more than four hours, you are covered for up to $500 for reasonable and eligible hotel, motel or restaurant expenses whenever you use your Card and/or Aeroplan miles to cover the full cost of your transportation.
  • Delayed and Lost Baggage Insurance12
    Shop for delayed items. If your baggage is delayed more than 6 hours, each insured person is covered for emergency purchases of essentials, such as clothing and toiletries. Coverage is also provided if your baggage is lost by a Common Carrier. Use your Card and/or Aeroplan miles to cover the full cost of the tickets and each insured person is covered up to an overall maximum of $1,000 per trip.
  • Common Carrier Travel Accident Insurance11
    You, your spouse and dependent children are insured up to $500,000 while travelling on a common carrier (for example, a bus, ferry, plane, or train) whenever you charge the full cost of the transportation to your Card and/or Aeroplan miles.

Auto Rental Collision/Loss Damage Insurance13

Drive protected. Use your Card and/or Aeroplan miles to pay for the full cost of an eligible car rental, and get the Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) coverage at no additional cost for up to 48 consecutive days. Please note, some rental agencies may require you to buy the CDW coverage from them. It is important to contact the rental agency before reserving a car to verify their policies.

Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection14

You automatically have access to Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection, which includes coverage for eligible items you’ve purchased with your Card:

  • Repair, replacement, or reimbursement should eligible items be stolen or damaged within 90 days of purchase
  • Double your warranty, up to 12 additional months if the eligible item came with a manufacturer's warranty valid in Canada

Be prepared with optional Balance Protection insurance15

Optional Balance Protection insurance products are designed to assist in managing your credit obligations on your TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Card in the event of your Involuntary Unemployment, Total Disability, Dismemberment, Critical Illness or Death.


To learn more about optional Balance Protection insurance products or to enroll, call 1-866-315-9069.

Explore your TD® Aeroplan®Visa Infinite* Card security features and protection

Protecting the security of your Card should always be a priority. That’s why your TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Card comes with features that help protect you when you shop online and in-person.

Security Features

  • Shop online and in person securely with Visa Zero Liability and be protected in the event that unauthorized transactions are made on your Card. See your Cardholder Agreement for complete information on unauthorized transactions and your responsibilities including your responsibility to protect your PIN.
  • Shop with confidence with chip & PIN technology. Chip and PIN technology provides an added level of security through the use of a Personal Identification Number (PIN).

Personal Identification Number (PIN) Security Tips

  • Choose a PIN you can remember that's difficult to guess. Don't use a phone number or birthday.
  • Never write down your PIN.
  • Protect your PIN and use your hand or body to shield the keypad when you enter your PIN.
  • Never let anyone else know or use your PIN or use your Card.
  • Make transactions only when and where you feel secure. If in doubt, don’t use your Card.

Reset your PIN easily and conveniently at a TD Canada Trust Branch or 24/7 at any TD Canada Trust Green Machine (ATM).

Fraud Protection (24 hours)

Our enhanced protection measures are part of an industry-wide effort to protect you from the inconvenience of credit card fraud. In the event that our advanced fraud prevention system detects unusual activity relating to your Account:

  • You may receive a phone call from a TD Credit Card Customer Service Representative asking you to confirm that you, or an Authorized User, performed a transaction on your TD Credit Card Account.
  • During a transaction, a merchant may receive an electronic message requesting that they contact the TD Credit Card Contact Centre and allow you to speak with one of our Customer Service Representatives.
  • Your Card may be temporarily blocked until we can contact you for verification.
  • You can now automatically receive TD Fraud Alerts to your mobile phone any time we suspect suspicious activity on your TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Card. Learn more

If you have any questions about fraud protection, please do not hesitate to call our Loss Prevention Centre immediately and speak to one of our Customer Service Representatives, 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
