Introducing the TD Skill for Amazon Alexa

Connect with TD through the sound of your voice, with Amazon Alexa.

To check in with TD from the comfort of your home, just start talking. Using only your voice, Amazon Alexa provides an easy, hands-free way to connect with TD through Alexa-enabled devices.

From sharing today’s U.S. Dollar exchange rate, to getting information about TD Credit Cards, Amazon Alexa is ready to help.

How to get started

  1. Get the Skill
    Open the Alexa app on your mobile device or go to Amazon.com, select “Skills” and search for “TD”
  2. Enable the Skill
    Now select “Enable”
  3. Start Talking
    Say “Alexa, open TD”

What you can do


Say, "Alexa, open TD" to start the conversation

Find us

Alexa can find you the nearest ATM or TD branch so you can come talk to us.




"Alexa, find me an ATM"

Get in touch

Alexa can tell you the best number to call - whether to report a lost or stolen TD Credit Card, or speak with us about your everyday banking needs.

"Alexa, what's the contact number for everyday banking?"

Get stock quotes

Alexa can get quotes for both Canadian and US stocks.

"Alexa, what is the stock price of TD"

Find exchange rates

Alexa can give exchange rates for buying or selling some of our most popular currencies, including U.S. Dollars, Pounds Sterling, Euros, Japanese Yen, Mexican Pesos, Australian Dollars, New Zealand Dollars and Swiss Francs.

"Alexa, what’s the exchange rate for buying U.S. Dollars?”

Learn about some of our available bank accounts and TD Credit Cards

Find out more about our products, from a range of chequing accounts, to the TD Cash Back Credit Card.

"Alexa, tell me about products"
