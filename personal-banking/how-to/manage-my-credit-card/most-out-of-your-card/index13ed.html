Pay your TD Credit Card Account automatically each month

You can arrange to have the monthly Minimum Payment or the balance in full automatically debited from your deposit account to pay your TD Credit Card Account. Payments can be made from your chequing or savings account at any financial institution.

Set up a Pre-Authorized Debit for your TD Credit Card:

  1. Read and complete the Pre-Authorized Debit (PAD) Agreement
  2. Mail or fax your completed Pre-Authorized Debit Agreement to:

    TD Canada Trust
    P.O. Box 337 STN A
    Orangeville ON L9W 9Z9

    Personal TD Credit Cards:
    1-877-941-4033 (Fax Number)

    Change/Cancel Pre-Authorized Debit set up:
    If you want to change the deposit account for the Pre-Authorized Debit set up for your TD Credit Card Account or you want to cancel the Pre-Authorized Debit set up for your TD Credit Card Account, just follow the same steps set out above.

Request a Credit Limit Increase

You may consider requesting a credit limit increase online through EasyWeb or through the TD app if using your mobile device. We may approve you for your requested amount or for an amount lower than you requested, or we may decline your request. We will advise you accordingly.

Note: Only the Primary Cardholder can make a request to increase the credit limit for a TD Credit Card Account and the Account must be open and in good standing.

Request through EasyWeb Online Banking

  • Register for EasyWeb Online banking
  • If you’re already registered, login to EasyWeb Online banking
  • Click on the TD Credit Card Account you wish to increase
  • On left menu, click Request a Credit Limit Increase
  • Follow the steps outlined to complete the request

Request through the TD app

  • Download the TD app for iOS (iPhone) or Android
  • If you already have access to the TD app, log in using your EasyWeb Login ID and password
  • Click on the TD Credit Card Account you wish to increase
  • On top menu click on More then click Request a Credit Limit Increase
  • Follow the steps outlined to complete the request

Request by Phone

You can request a credit limit increase by phone by calling 1-800-983-2582, seven days a week, 7 a.m. to 12 midnight (ET).

If your request is approved, or if you are approved for a lower amount than requested, your new credit limit will be reflected in your Online banking profile within 2 business days following our receipt of your request.

If your request is not approved, we will send you written confirmation of this decline by mail to the mailing address we have on file for you, within 7-10 business days from the date of your request.

Keep your personal information up-to-date

Here’s how to update your information online:
  • Login to EasyWeb Online banking.
  • Select Profile & Settings from the left menu.
  • Select My Profile
  • Use the respective Edit links to change your address, phone number and email.

Important note: If your Credit Card statement on EasyWeb Online banking does not reflect your updated address, call us at 1-800-983-8472

If you need assistance updating your personal information call EasyLine 1-866-222-3456

1Subject to available credit. Balance Transfers are treated as Cash Advances and accrue interest at the Cash Advance rate that applies to your Account from the date the Balance Transfer is made.

Lost or stolen cards

If your TD Credit Card is lost or stolen, please contact TD Credit Cards Customer Service at 1-800-983-8472 and a representative will be happy to assist you.

Damaged card replacement request

If your TD Credit Card is damaged or not working, you can request a replacement card online through EasyWeb, through the TD app using your mobile device, or by phone at 1-800-983-8472.

Request through EasyWeb Online Banking

  • Register for EasyWeb Online banking if you have not already done so
  • If you’re already registered, login to EasyWeb Online banking using your EasyWeb Login ID and password
  • Select the credit card you want to replace
  • On left menu under View Accounts, click Replace a Damaged Card
  • Follow the steps outlined to complete the request

Request through the TD app

Note: You must be an EasyWeb online banking user to download the TD app.

  • Download the TD app for iOS (iPhone) or Android to your mobile device.
  • If you have already downloaded the TD app, log in using your EasyWeb Login ID and password
  • Select the credit card you want to replace
  • On top menu, tap the More button then select Replace a Damaged Card
  • Follow the steps outlined to complete the request

Request by Phone

You can request to replace a damaged card by phone by calling 1-800-983-8472.
